## Approach 1: Min Heap (Recommended)

<ApproachTag tag="recommended" />

Maintain a min heap of size k containing the k largest elements. Iterate through the array and keep only the k largest elements in the heap. The root of the heap is the kth largest.

<ComplexityBadges time="O(n log k)" space="O(k)" timeColor="green" spaceColor="yellow" timeNote="n iterations, each O(log k)" spaceNote="Heap of k elements" />
